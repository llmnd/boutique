# Privacy Policy - Boutique Mobile

**Last Updated:** November 26, 2025  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025

## 1. Introduction

Boutique Mobile ("App", "we", "us", or "our") operates the Boutique Mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

We use your data to provide and improve the Service. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 2. Information Collection and Use

### 2.1 Information We Collect

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you:

#### Personal Data:
- **Email Address:** When you register an account, we collect your email for authentication and account recovery
- **Phone Number:** Optional, used for contact purposes
- **Account Name:** To personalize your experience
- **Client Information:** Names and contact numbers of clients you manage in the app

#### Financial Data:
- **Debt/Loan Records:** Amounts, dates, descriptions of debts and loans you track
- **Payment History:** Records of payments made and received
- **Statistics:** Aggregated financial summaries and reports

#### Technical Data:
- **Device Information:** Device type, OS version, unique identifiers
- **Usage Data:** Features used, time spent in app, crash reports
- **Authentication Tokens:** Temporarily stored for secure session management
- **PIN Hash:** Encrypted PIN for local authentication (never transmitted)

### 2.2 How We Use Information

We use collected information for various purposes:
- To provide and maintain the Service
- To notify you about changes to our Service
- To provide customer care and support
- To gather analysis or valuable information so that we can improve the Service
- To monitor the usage of the Service
- To detect, prevent and address technical and security issues

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Local Storage
- All debt, loan, and client data is primarily stored locally on your device using Hive database
- Your PIN is hashed locally and never transmitted to servers
- Financial records are encrypted at rest on your device

### 3.2 Server Storage
- Authentication tokens and user session data are stored securely on our servers
- Personal identification information is encrypted and secured
- We use industry-standard SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit

### 3.3 Security Measures
- Encrypted local database (Hive)
- Secure PIN authentication
- HTTPS-only server communication
- Regular security audits
- We do not store complete financial transaction details on servers

## 4. Offline Functionality

The App is designed to work offline:
- All data is synced to your device's local storage
- You can use the App without internet connection
- When reconnected, data automatically syncs securely
- No personal data is required to use offline features

## 5. Data Retention

- **Personal Account Data:** Retained while your account is active, deleted upon account deletion
- **Financial Records:** Retained as long as you maintain the App, deleted when you uninstall
- **Temporary Tokens:** Automatically expired after 24 hours of inactivity
- **Usage Logs:** Retained for 90 days for support and analytics purposes

## 6. Third-Party Services

### 3rd Party Libraries Used:
- **http:** For secure network requests
- **shared_preferences:** For local preference storage
- **sqflite:** Local database
- **hive:** Encrypted local storage
- **connectivity_plus:** Network status detection
- **pdf:** PDF report generation (processed locally)

These libraries do not collect or transmit personal data to third-party servers.

## 7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with Personal Data, please contact us immediately.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.

## Summary for App Stores

**For Google Play Store:**
This app collects personal financial data (debts, loans, payments) which is primarily stored locally on your device. Email is required for account authentication. All data is encrypted locally using Hive database. The PIN is hashed locally and never transmitted. The app works fully offline and syncs data securely when reconnected. We do not sell or share your financial data with third parties.

**For Apple App Store:**
Boutique Mobile stores personal financial management data locally on your device with optional cloud sync. We collect minimal personal data (email for authentication). Financial records are encrypted locally. We respect your privacy and do not share data with third parties. CCPA and GDPR compliant.
